Our Story

About HigherGrade Tutoring

Built by students, for students. This is math the way we always wished it was taught.

Our Mission

Math the way it should feel

HigherGrade Tutoring was born from a simple frustration: math in school too often feels like a chore. We knew it didn't have to be that way.

A group of Grade 11 and 12 HDSB students decided to do something about it. We designed a summer camp from scratch — the sessions, the problem sets, the exams, the whole experience — because we wanted a math environment that we would actually want to attend when we were in Grade 9.

No adults scripting things. No rigid curriculum. Just curious students exploring fascinating ideas together, led entirely by their peers.

What We Stand For

Our values

🌱

Student Ownership

From planning to execution, every decision is made by students. This camp is a reflection of what we actually find meaningful in math.

🤝

Radical Inclusion

Free to attend. Built around the HDSB MTH1W curriculum, but students from any school board are welcome to apply. Strong fundamentals and motivation are the prerequisites.

🔥

Genuine Passion

We only teach what excites us. Every topic, every problem set, every activity was chosen because someone on our team is genuinely passionate about it.

Our principles
