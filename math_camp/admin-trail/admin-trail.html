Upload a backdrop for the trail, drop in custom log images for each stage, and drag the 5 path dots to plot exactly where the camper walks before each question appears.

🖼 Trail backdrop

A single image that tiles vertically beneath every stage. When set, the trail switches to a cozy 1/7-screen-wide layout, stages shrink so there's less walking distance between logs, and the procedural grass scenery is hidden so your backdrop owns the look.

Drop an image or click below to choose one.

🌲 Stages

53 stages total. For each one you can upload a custom log image, drag 5 path dots, and (when the stage unlocks a paper crane) drag the crane's hiding spot.
Blue dots 1–4 are the path the character walks. Gold dot is the log (waypoint 5 — where the question appears). Pink crane dot only shows on stages whose answer unlocks a letter; drag it to choose where the hidden crane appears for campers to find.
